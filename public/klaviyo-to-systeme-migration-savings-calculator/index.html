Why Klaviyo costs so much — and what to do about it

Klaviyo changed its billing model in February 2025, charging for every active profile whether or not you send to them. For stores with growing lists, this means costs that scale relentlessly regardless of how actively you use the platform.

The best free Klaviyo alternatives in 2026

Systeme.io's permanently free plan covers up to 2,000 contacts with unlimited email sends, automated flows, sales funnels, and a CRM — replicating Klaviyo's core functionality at zero cost. For stores with larger lists, GetResponse offers a competitive free entry tier with more advanced segmentation than Systeme.io.

What you can and can't replicate for free

Welcome sequences, abandoned cart emails, post-purchase flows, and basic segmentation all migrate directly. Advanced predictive analytics, Klaviyo's Shopify-native browsing abandonment triggers, and SMS marketing do not have direct free equivalents — though Make.com can replicate browsing abandonment via webhooks with some setup work.
