What are Shopify Agentic Storefronts?

Launched in Shopify's Winter '26 Edition, Agentic Storefronts allow customers to discover and purchase products directly inside AI platforms — ChatGPT, Google AI Mode, Microsoft Copilot — without leaving the conversation. The AI agent handles product discovery, questions, and checkout entirely within the chat interface. As of April 2026 this is in early access for US-based stores only.

Why server-side tracking is critical for agentic orders

Checkout inside an AI platform does not trigger client-side pixels. Your Meta pixel, GA4 browser tag, and TikTok pixel will not fire for any purchase completed through an agentic storefront. Server-side CAPI is the only way to capture these conversion events in your ad platform data — making it a prerequisite, not an optional enhancement, for any store planning to use agentic commerce.

How to prepare your store now

Enable guest checkout, complete all store policies (Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Refund Policy), and implement true server-side tracking before applying for early access. Stores with subscriptions or age-restricted products need to wait for expanded eligibility.
