Frequently asked questions

What are Shopify Agentic Storefronts?
Launched in Shopify's Winter '26 Edition, Agentic Storefronts allow customers to discover and purchase your products directly inside AI platforms like ChatGPT, Google AI Mode, and Microsoft Copilot without leaving the conversation.
Which stores are eligible for Agentic Storefronts?
As of April 2026, eligibility is limited to US-based stores selling to US customers. Guest checkout must be enabled, all store policies must be added, and subscriptions and age-restricted products are currently not supported.
Do Agentic Storefront orders fire tracking pixels?
No. Checkout inside AI platforms does not trigger client-side pixels. Server-side tracking via CAPI is essential to capture these orders in your ad platform data.
