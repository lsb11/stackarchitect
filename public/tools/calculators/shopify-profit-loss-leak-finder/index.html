Frequently asked questions

What is a good net profit margin for a Shopify store?
A net margin above 15% is considered healthy for most Shopify stores. Above 20% is excellent. Below 10% is a warning sign that one or more cost categories need immediate attention.
Are Shopify app fees eating into my margins?
For the average mid-sized Shopify store, app fees represent 1–4% of revenue. At £300–£600/month in app costs on a £15,000/month revenue store, that is 2–4% of net margin going to software — often replaceable with free alternatives.
