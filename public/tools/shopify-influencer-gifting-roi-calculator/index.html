Is influencer gifting actually profitable for Shopify stores?

Influencer gifting is one of the most popular DTC marketing tactics — and one of the least rigorously measured. The real question is not whether you got coverage, but whether the coverage generated enough revenue to justify the product cost, shipping, and time investment at your store's margin level.

Micro vs macro influencer economics

Micro-influencers (5k–20k followers) consistently outperform macro influencers on ROI because engagement rates are 3–5x higher despite smaller reach. A gifting programme sending 20 products to micro-influencers typically delivers better cost-per-acquisition than 2 products to macro influencers at the same total cost.

What conversion rate to expect

Industry benchmarks suggest 0.3–0.8% of an influencer's audience will visit your store from a single post. Of those visitors, your normal store conversion rate applies — typically 1–3%. For a 10k follower micro-influencer, expect 30–80 visits and 1–2 sales per post. Volume and repetition (multiple posts per influencer) dramatically improves these numbers.
