Frequently asked questions

Is influencer gifting profitable for Shopify stores?
It depends heavily on follower engagement and product margins. Micro-influencer gifting (5k–20k followers) typically shows better ROI than macro partnerships because engagement rates are 3–5x higher despite smaller reach.
What conversion rate should I expect from influencer gifting?
Industry benchmarks suggest 0.3–0.8% of an influencer's audience will visit your store from a single post. Of those, your normal store conversion rate applies (typically 1–3%). So for a 20k follower influencer, expect 60–160 visits and 1–5 sales per post.
