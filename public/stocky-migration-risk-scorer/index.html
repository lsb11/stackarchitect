What's happening with Shopify Stocky?

Shopify is discontinuing Stocky on August 31, 2026. Every merchant currently using Stocky for purchase orders, inventory management, demand forecasting, or supplier tracking needs to migrate to an alternative before that date. Data may not be recoverable after the shutdown.

Your migration options

For stores with under 200 SKUs and straightforward supplier relationships, a Google Sheets-based system using Make.com automations replicates all core Stocky features at zero cost. Stack Architect's Stocky Swap is a pre-built version of this that deploys in under 4 minutes. For complex operations with 500+ SKUs and multiple suppliers, a dedicated inventory app (Cin7, Unleashed, or Katana) will be more appropriate.

What to do before August 31

Export all purchase orders, supplier data, inventory counts, and historical demand data from Stocky now, regardless of which replacement you choose. Shopify has confirmed data may not be accessible after the app closes.
