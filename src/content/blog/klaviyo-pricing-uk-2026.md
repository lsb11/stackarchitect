# Klaviyo Pricing UK 2026 — Exact £ Costs, VAT, and Free Alternatives

Klaviyo charges in US dollars. UK Shopify stores pay in pounds at the live exchange rate — and then pay 20% VAT on top. This guide gives you the exact £ costs at every tier, explains why UK bills increased sharply in 2025, and covers the best alternatives for UK stores that want to cut their email marketing costs significantly or entirely.

## Klaviyo Pricing in £ for UK Stores (2026)

Klaviyo bills in USD. The following table converts at an approximate rate of **£1 = $1.27** — the approximate GBP/USD rate through early 2026. Your actual cost will vary with exchange rate movements.

| Contacts | USD/month | GBP/month (approx) | GBP/month incl. 20% VAT |
|---|---|---|---|
| Up to 500 | $20 | £16 | **£19** |
| Up to 1,000 | $30 | £24 | **£28** |
| Up to 5,000 | $100 | £79 | **£95** |
| Up to 10,000 | $175 | £138 | **£165** |
| Up to 25,000 | $400 | £315 | **£378** |
| Up to 50,000 | $720 | £567 | **£680** |
| Up to 100,000 | $1,100 | £866 | **£1,040** |

**VAT note:** UK businesses are charged VAT at 20% on Klaviyo subscriptions under HMRC digital services rules. VAT-registered businesses can reclaim this on their quarterly VAT return, but cash flow implications are real — you pay upfront and reclaim later.

## Why UK Klaviyo Bills Increased in 2025

Two things happened simultaneously that hit UK stores harder than US stores:

**1. The active profiles billing change (February 2025)**

Klaviyo switched from billing based on email volume to billing based on total active profiles — every contact in your account, whether you email them or not. This disproportionately affected UK stores because GDPR compliance often creates large suppressed segments: contacts who didn't re-consent during post-Brexit list cleans, unsubscribes, bounces, and lapsed customers that UK stores keep for legal compliance periods rather than deleting. All of these now count toward your billable total.

**2. GBP/USD exchange rate pressure**

Sterling weakness against the dollar through 2024–2025 meant that the same USD price cost meaningfully more in pounds. A store on the $400/month plan paid approximately £320/month in 2022 and approximately £378/month including VAT in 2026 — a 20%+ increase with no change in their Klaviyo plan.

## Klaviyo Free Plan — What UK Stores Actually Get

Klaviyo's free plan covers **up to 250 contacts** with a cap of 500 emails per month. For most UK Shopify stores, this is effectively unusable — a single welcome sequence for new signups will hit the send cap within days.

The 250-contact free tier is designed as a trial, not a sustainable free option. Most stores exceed it within their first month of using Klaviyo.

Both Systeme.io and GetResponse maintain GDPR and UK GDPR compliance. All three platforms support double opt-in, consent recording, and data subject access request workflows required by the ICO. When migrating from Klaviyo, your existing consent records transfer with your contact export — you do not need to re-obtain consent from existing opted-in subscribers.

Systeme.io on the free plan eliminates currency risk entirely. For paid alternatives, GetResponse prices in USD but is generally more cost-stable than Klaviyo due to simpler pricing structure. Pricing your email marketing in GBP where possible removes one variable from your operating cost base.

**Making Tax Digital:**
Klaviyo invoices are issued in USD. For UK VAT-registered businesses, you will need to convert these to GBP on each invoice date for your VAT records. Systeme.io on the free plan generates no invoices and no MTD obligations for that cost centre.

## Should UK Stores Stay on Klaviyo?

Klaviyo is worth retaining if your store genuinely uses its advanced ecommerce features — predictive CLV, deep behavioural segmentation based on Shopify purchase history, or the Klaviyo AI product recommendations. These are genuinely differentiated from Systeme.io.

Klaviyo is not worth the cost if you are primarily using it for: welcome sequences, abandoned cart emails, post-purchase flows, and occasional broadcast campaigns. All of these are available free on Systeme.io with no material difference in deliverability or conversion rates for most UK stores.
