Systeme.io provides **2,000 contacts and unlimited email sends permanently free** — plus sales funnels, CRM, and affiliate management that Klaviyo never included. For stores over 2,000 contacts, GetResponse delivers equivalent automation at 60–70% less than Klaviyo's current pricing.

## Why Klaviyo Bills Are Rising in 2026

Klaviyo changed its billing model in February 2025. Previously you paid based on contacts you actively emailed each month. Now you pay for **all active profiles** — every contact in your account whether you email them or not. [Klaviyo's own pricing documentation](https://www.klaviyo.com/pricing) confirms that active profiles include any contact not manually suppressed, regardless of engagement history.

Unengaged subscribers who signed up for a discount code two years ago and never bought. People who opened one email and never returned. All of them count toward your billing tier.

The compounding effect: as you run ads, collect emails at checkout, and grow your list through popups, your Klaviyo bill climbs automatically — even when your revenue doesn't.

None of these replace what Klaviyo does at any meaningful scale.

## How to Migrate from Klaviyo to Systeme.io in 30 Minutes

**Step 1 — Create your Systeme.io account**

Go to [Systeme.io](/go/systeme) and sign up on the free plan. No credit card required.

**Step 2 — Export your Klaviyo contact list**

In Klaviyo: **Profiles → All Profiles → Export**. Download as CSV. Include email address, first name, last name, and any custom properties you want to preserve.

**Step 3 — Import contacts to Systeme.io**

In Systeme.io: **Contacts → Import Contacts**. Upload your CSV and map the columns. Add a tag like `imported-from-klaviyo` to segment this group. Large lists import in under 5 minutes.

**Step 4 — Rebuild your key automations**

In Systeme.io's automation builder, recreate your three core flows:

- **Welcome sequence:** trigger on contact creation, send 3–5 emails over 7–10 days introducing your brand and products
- **Abandoned cart:** trigger via Shopify webhook (set up through [Make.com](/go/make)), send 2–3 follow-up emails within 24 hours
- **Post-purchase:** trigger on order completion, send thank you, onboarding, and review request sequences

Most stores need 2–3 core automations. Each takes 15–20 minutes to rebuild.

**Step 5 — Connect Shopify**

In Systeme.io: **Settings → Integrations → Shopify**. Use the native integration for basic order triggers. For more advanced abandoned cart and segmentation by purchase behaviour, connect via a [Make.com webhook](/blog/make-com-shopify-automation-guide) — this gives you real-time order data and full control over trigger logic.

**Step 6 — Test and cancel Klaviyo**

Send a test through each automation and verify delivery. Once confirmed, cancel Klaviyo: **Account → Plan → Cancel Plan**. Your data remains accessible for 90 days after cancellation.
